# EventHub Product Context

EventHub is a product UI for a small cultural association that manages events about watch culture, concerts, workshops and book presentations. The primary users are attendees who search events and manage tickets, organizers who publish events and inspect registrations, and admins who moderate the platform.

Register: product.

The interface should feel operational, clear and trustworthy. It should avoid marketing-heavy hero pages and instead place the event catalog, ticket state, organizer work and moderation workflows directly in front of the user.
